Activity Ideas:

1. Plan a healthy picnic, whether at one of our state park picnic areas or on a blanket in your grass.
2. Plan a nature scavenger hunt. They can be done just about anywhere – even in the tiniest of yards or green space. A simple Google search results in hundreds of ideas.
3. Just open the door! You will be surprised what activities your children will come up with simply by being in the outdoors and allowing their imaginations to take over.

Biking

Nature lovers, extreme adventurers and those with a need for speed all appreciate the stunning biking trails in Colorado's state parks! We've got it all - relaxing and scenic rides, great odds of spotting wildlife, and excellent opportunities for a major workout!​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​
